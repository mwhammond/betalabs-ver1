Our mission

Imperial Innovations creates, builds and invests in high-potential startups. We believe that there is a growing challenge across the Innovation community; that of bringing the speed and energy of start-ups and the experience and resources of industry together in an efficient and effective manner.

Accelerators and venture investors have traditionally made these connections however this process is far from optimal. Recognising that there is clear demand from both sides we are testing out the potential for a more scalable online solution. Sound exciting? Get involved.
