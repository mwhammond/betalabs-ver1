Why are we building this?

Imperial Innovations creates, builds and invests in high-potential startups. We believe that there are many industry sectors that are ripe for innovation but have little interaction with start-up and research communities - a more direct link would benefit both sides both at both an individual career level through to the industry as a whole. As investors we see this problem on a daily basis and decided to create a more efficient solution.

How does it work?

We do not advertise jobs, we create introductions that might develop into a formal advisory or even full time role. Once a match is found you will be alerted and if both sides demonstrate interest you will be able to contact each other directly. In the very near future we will enable the ability to browse opportunities and experts on the site and recieve regular updates.
